My favourite hobby is dancing. I have been dancing since I was 4 years old and have been in the same dance class. Dancing makes me happy and I love all dance styles. It also helps in keeping our body fit.

More about dance:
Dance is a performing art form consisting of purposefully selected sequences of human movement. This movement has aesthetic and symbolic value, and is acknowledged as dance by performers and observers within a particular culture. Dance can be categorized and described by its choreography, by its repertoire of movements, or by its historical period or place of origin.
